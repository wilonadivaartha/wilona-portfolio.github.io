SMP Swasta Roma Katolik Budi Mulia

Budi Mulia Private Junior High School Pangururan is a school under the auspices of the Budi Mulia Lourdes Foundation, which is managed by the Brothers of St. Mary of Lourdes (Budi Mulia). This school was initiated and founded on July 1, 1953, through the initiative and invitation of Pastor Raudboud Watereus, a priest who worked in the Pangururan Parish, to the Congregation of the Brothers of Budi Mulia. The school was established with the aim of meeting the fundamental educational needs of the community, especially in Pangururan, Samosir Regency, North Sumatra Province.

Institut Teknologi Del

Vocational education has been the first level of education at the Del Institute of Technology since its establishment in 2001 (formerly known as the Del Informatics Polytechnic). Vocational education is a higher education focusing on practical work that can support expertise in specific fields of study. The vocational program aims to prepare its graduates to become a workforce that is job-ready and capable of competing globally in the Business and Industrial World (DUDI).
